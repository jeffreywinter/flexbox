Jeff Winter

Writer, Reporter, Producer

Winter grew up in Chicago, studied English and Film at the University of Iowa, and now lives in Flatbush, Brooklyn.

As a student at CUNY's Craig Newmarkl Graduate School of Journalism, he studies law enforcement, urban history and housing. He hopes to become an investigative reporter upon graduation.

About Winter

Winter loves city politics. He writes about East New York, cops, and other urban issues in New York City.

Winter is obsessed by New York City. He loves exploring his city and meeting its people. This summer has taken him to Breezy Point to Cypress Hills.

Winter works for Business Administration at CNN. He has produced segments on teacher strikes and media mergers, among others.
